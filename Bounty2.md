## Getting started

Building dApps on LUKSO is easy because it is the same as building dApps for any EVM chains. The main difference is that LUKSO users use smart accounts (Universal Profiles) through our [UP Browser Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/universal-profiles/abpickdkkbnbcoepogfhkhennhfhehfn) to interact with your dApp: this unlocks a lot of great possibilities âœ¨
